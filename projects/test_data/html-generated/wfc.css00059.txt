      <div class="salute">My. Dear. Mollie </div>
      
      
      
      <p>I will arrive in St Louis May 3th. Now I think it will be just as well for you to
         arrive there about the same time go to the St James Hotel if I dont Meet you at the
         Depot. and let me know you are there I hope you are well and happy. I have the white
         horse for you—
      </p>
      
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <div class="salute">With Love </div>
         
         
         <br></br>W. F. C.
