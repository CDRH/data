         <div class="dateline">February 27, 1926. </div>
         
         
         Mr. John G. Neihardt,
         Branson, Mo.
         
         
         
         <div class="salute">Dear Mr. Neihardt: </div>
         
         
         <p>In one of your recent letters, you suggested that we ought to get up a little booklet
            on THE SONG OF THE INDIAN WARS, and you offered to prepare the material. I would be
            very glad if you would do this, and will see that prompt use is made of it in our
            spring campaign on your work.
         </p>
         
         <div class="closer">
            
            <div class="salute">Sincerely yours, </div>
            
            
            <br></br>H.S. Latham
            <br></br>
            K
            
            
            <div class="note">HSL.K</div>
             
         </div>
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class=" ">
         
         
         <div class="salute">Dearest Comrade: </div>
         
         
         <p>I'll furnish the press clippings for this booklet. Would you have time to do a brief
            
            <del class=""></del>article (say 300 words) to be used in the booklet, and covering the significance of
            the three volumes of the cycle published, with especial emphasis on the 
            <u>Wars</u>? If so, I should have the stuff very soon.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>I'm jumping sidewise with my page for the Journal-Post, but am pushing 
            <u>Messiah</u> too, - faster than usual. Might mention Collected Poems to be published in the Fall
            &amp; comment on scope &amp; volume thereof.
         </p>
         
         <div class="closer">
            
            <div class="salute">Love, </div> 
            <br></br>Jno
             
         </div>
         
      </div>       
      
      
      <div class="note">I'll answer your good letter soon. So glad Mary is Mary House again.</div>       
      
      
      <div class="note">
         <u>Latest Idea:</u> I think, if you can write the brief article, it should be sent direct to H.S. Latham
         by you. I'll send the other stuff from here.
      </div>  
      
      <div class="note">You would probably remark 
         <span class="">[in passing?]</span> that 
         <u>Friends</u> + Glass are already "classics" spreading widely in the schools
