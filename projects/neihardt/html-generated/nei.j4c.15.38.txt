      <div class="dateline">Apr. 10, '31. </div>
      
      
      
      
      
      <div class="salute">Dear Comrade: </div>
      
      
      <p>I've been thinking a great deal of you, and I'm wondering if the bad arm is giving
         you a lot of hell. I do hope not.
      </p>
      
      
      <p>It would be great if you could be free to go north with Enid, Hilda, and me in May.
         I suppose this is not possible. A comfortable fast car &amp; camping! Not like our Ford
         journey!
      </p>
      
      
      <p>This carries love as always.</p>
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <br></br>Jno.
          
      </div>
