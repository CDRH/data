SKYRIM FARM

ROUTE 7

COLUMBIA, MISSOURI
5-11-'58
Dear Lucile & Bower:

Thanks for the invitation to be a guest in your home after June 8. I will return from Yankton about June 7, and then I can come to you for a week or more. I think this is a good plan, as it will save you much coming and going. I do hope, however, that we may be at Skyrim once or twice. If we give our time to the job, much can be done in even one week.

75
