<h6 class="fw">27</h6>


<span class="dateline">Branson, Mo., May 27, '21.</span>

<span class="salute">Dear Seymour:—</span>

<p>Thanks for the good letter and for the manuscript. It would be a good idea to substitute Mrs. Avery's Bookfellow poem for the one I included in the Pack. I can see that you had a bully time on the 14th!</p>

<span class="closer">
<span class="salute">Yours always,</span>

<br/>Jno. N.
