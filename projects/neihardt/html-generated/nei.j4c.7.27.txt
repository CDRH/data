      <div class="salute">Dear Seymour:— </div>
      
      
      <p>Thanks for the good letter and for the manuscript. It would be a good idea to substitute
         Mrs. Avery's Bookfellow poem for the one I included in the Pack. I can see that you
         had a bully time on the 14th!
      </p>

         <div class="salute">Yours always, </div>
