Branson, Mo., Oct. 14, '24.
Dear Mott: -

I'm just leaving for a month's lecture trip, and I want to tell you something before I go. Macmillans have just written me, proposing the publication of my collected poems in one volume next fall. This pleases me, naturally, and I feel sure it will please you. It will be a volume of 700 pages.

I was very glad to hear from you and to know that you have completed your work for the doctorate. Congratulations!

With kind thoughts,


Jno. Neihardt
