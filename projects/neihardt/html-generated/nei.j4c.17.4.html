Mr. Clark McAdams Post-Dispatch St. Louis, Mo.
Dear Mr. McAdams:

It was impossible to get another leader to you Friday yesterday morning. This did not occur to me while we were talking on the 'phone Thursday afternoon. I hope it will be possible to use the enclosed this week. I know Gottlieb will see that the proof is correct.

I'm really sorry that the Masters article seems objectionable, but I'm sure you have good reasons. Never was a time more thoroughly bedevelled with false false prophets in all fields, and it seems worthwhile to help a little, whenever possible, to discredit them.

I was serious about your dropping in here and being around with us. You'd like it, I hope, and we would, I know.

Yours,


John G. Neihardt
