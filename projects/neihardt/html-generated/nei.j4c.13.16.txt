      <div class="salute">Dear Comrade: </div>
      
      
      <p>I suggest that you use these three articles under the general heading, "The Contemporary
         Trend in Literature". The order of articles should be as follows: Misleading Labels,
         Literature and the Unlettered, The Split Ray. Have the general head large, and the
         article headings smaller. This is a much better contribution from me than anything
         I'd do on any special writer.
      </p>
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <div class="salute">Endless love, </div>
         
         
         <br></br>Jno.
          
      </div>
      
      <p>I thought Stuff's article competent and earnest and quite worth while.</p>
      
      
      <p>Can you send me proofs?</p>
