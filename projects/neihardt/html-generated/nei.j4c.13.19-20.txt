<span class="dateline">Sept. 15, 1928.</span>

<span class="salute">Dear Comrade:</span>

<p>I am to read for Dana Hall Sunday, November 11th, Wellesley, Tuesday November 13th, and Boston City Club and Boston University within two or three days of these dates. Could I get to Boston for Sunday evening engagement if I should stop with you for Friday stunt?</p>
<span class="closer">
<span class="salute">With love always,</span>

<br/>Jno.
</span>
 <div class="note">Did you hear of the new Hall at Wayne State?</div>
