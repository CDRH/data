                <span class="salute">Dear Seymours:</span>
                
                <p>Hilda plans to get away next week, and she will reach Chicago in the afternoon of Wednesday or Thursday. She will phone you.</p>
                
                <p>It's dear and characteristic of you to have Hilda. I know how she will feel about you two, and I do think you'll like her.</p>
                <span class="closer">
                <span class="salute">Kindest thoughts,</span>
