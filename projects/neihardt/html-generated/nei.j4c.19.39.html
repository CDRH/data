February 8, 1943.

Dear Seymours:

Hilda plans to get away next week, and she will reach Chicago in the afternoon of Wednesday or Thursday. She will phone you.

It's dear and characteristic of you to have Hilda. I know how she will feel about you two, and I do think you'll like her.

Kindest thoughts,


Jno. G. Neihardt
