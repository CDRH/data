Dear Comrade:

I am sending you a book on Hitler, which seems important.

You will be free to speak out about this, because you will in no way be attacking our system. You may have as much as 450 words. Sorry I can't allow more.

I suppose it would be impossible to have the review here by next Tuesday or Wednesday; if so, let me have it for the next week.

The book should be returned to the Literary Editor, Post-Dispatch, by express collect, not using my name. In this way, I will not have to pay the carriage charges.

When the article is printed, you will receive the check.

With love always,


Jno.
