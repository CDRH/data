                <span class="salute">Dear Pal:</span>
                
                <p>It was good to hear from you again and to know that you liked the Alys. They <u>are</u> very fine people — and very able too. Mrs. Aly wrote glowingly about you, John. She mentioned "the twinkle in your face" when you talked to her. I remember that "twinkle", John; and I'm glad it is still there! Mrs. Aly also wrote <u>most appreciatively</u> of Edna and her husband. I'm so glad you all liked each other.</p>
                <h6 class="fw">53</h6>
                
                <span class="hr"> </span><span class="pageimage"><a href="large/nei.j4c.21.53-54_0001a.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]" title="&lt;a href=&#34;large/nei.j4c.21.53-54_0001a.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;"><img src="thumbnails/nei.j4c.21.53-54_0001a.jpg" class="display &#xA;            "/></a></span>
                <h6 class="fw">-2-</h6>
                
                <p>Yes, I remember the diving into muddy Logan Creek — and I remember a heck of a lot more too!</p>
                
                <p>I appreciate your wish to pay for the <u>Cycle</u>, but it's a gift for old time's sake, John. You are a piece of my life, you know! I'm returning the  bill.</p>	
                <span class="closer">
                <span class="salute">With the old affection,</span>
                
                <br/>Jno. N.   
                </span>
                
                <p>To --
                    John E. Chaffee,
                    -Albany, Oregon,</p>
