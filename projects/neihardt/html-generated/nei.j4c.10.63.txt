<span class="salute">Dear Comrade:-</span>

<p>That's a corking good job of writing, your Goldenrod letter, published Oct 26. I had said so to myself long before I reached the end.</p>
<span class="closer">
<span class="salute">Love,</span>

<br/>Jno
</span>
