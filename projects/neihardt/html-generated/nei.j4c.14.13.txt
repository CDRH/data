      <div class="salute">Dear Comrade: </div>
      
      
      <p>If you haven't Laski's "The Dangers of Obedience", I suggest that you get it from
         Harpers and review it in your magazine. Might be a good idea to hook it up with Tawney's
         "Acquisitive Society", which is now about 8 years old.
      </p>
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <div class="salute">With love, </div>
         
         <br></br>Jno.
