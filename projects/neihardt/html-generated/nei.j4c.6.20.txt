<h6 class="fw">20</h6>

<div class="note">Answered 10/8/'20</div>



<span class="dateline">Bancroft, Nebraska, Oct. 2, '20.</span>

<span class="salute">Dear Mr. Braithwaite: -</span>


	<p>I have a letter from our friend, Mr. Ledoux, in which he refers to some suggestion of yours regarding the possibility of making moving pictures out of the "Friends" and "Hugh Glass".</p>
	<p>I am writing this note to assure you that any suggestion coming from you would find me hospitable.</p>

<span class="closer">
<span class="salute">With all kind thoughts,</span>

<span class="salute">Yours,</span>

<br/>Jno G. Neihardt
