      <div class="salute">Dear Comrade: </div>
      
      
      <p>Bully to hear from you again! If I have seemed less than friendly, it was only seeming.
         I've been soused in my work and very happy.
      </p>
      
      
      <p>Tell the young lady, please, that she should not complete her thesis before July,
         when she could have a proof of The Song of the Messiah to study. It will appear in
         September. Just now I'm doing the few last lines. I'm grateful
      </p>
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <div class="salute">With the old affection, </div>
         
         <br></br>Jno.
          
      </div>
