      <div class="dateline">
         <div class="note">January 23, 1932</div> 
      </div>
      
      <div class="salute">Dear Comrade: </div>
      
      
      <p>I believe that a series of articles on your pioneer experiences would make an excellent
         feature for the magazine, &amp; I hope you will go ahead. Such memories are decidedly
         worth recording and you will do it with a warmth of feeling that will make them come
         alive.
      </p>
      
      
      <p>The enclosed shows how Morrows are going at their job. You may shove it into your
         next letter.
      </p>
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <div class="salute">Love always </div>
         
         
         <br></br>Jno.
          
      </div>
