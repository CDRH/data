<span class="dateline">Bancroft, Nebraska, April 19, '11.</span>


Thomas B. Mosher, Esq.,
Portland, Maine.

<span class="salute">Dear Sir:—</span>


<p>Several months ago Mr. Mitchell Kennerley wrote me that you had decided to bring out a number of The Bibelot containing a selection from my lyrics. Kindly advise me as to when this number may be expected.</p>
<span class="closer">
<span class="salute">Very truly yours,</span>

<br/>Jno. G. Neihardt
