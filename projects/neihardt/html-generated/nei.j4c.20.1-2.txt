                <span class="salute">Dear Dr. Kendrick:</span>
                
                <p>Many, many thanks for the <u>Book</u>! And what a book it is! I'm reading with real pleasure and profit, for I know you are master of the subject. I admire the way in which you have organized your knowledge, and the clarity with which you present it.</p>
