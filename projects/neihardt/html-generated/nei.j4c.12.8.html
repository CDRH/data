Editorial Department ST, LOUIS POST-DISPATCH

ST. LOUIS, MO.
Feb. 27 '27
Dearest Comrade:

Don't worry about this moment in a silly but necessary social experiment. Of course the rabble isn't educable.

We are doing something - you and I - that will outlast this twilight.

Signs of the growing success of the scheme are increasing.

I'm glad you're in the world with me.

Endless love,


Jno.
St. Louis Mo. Feb 27 12-PM 1927 REGISTER OR INSURE VALUABLE MAIL
