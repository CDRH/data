                <span class="salute">Dear Ones!</span>
           
                <p>It was a pleasant flight all the way, and I was cheated out of my $52,500 insurance — a fact which irks me a bit! But I almost beat the insurance company. We arrived at Columbia Air Port on time; but when we touched the field, it seemed to the pilot that the landing gear was hay-wire. The alarm light started going and the thing said 'beep-beep-beep'. So we circled Columbia for a half hour, hoping to shake the landing gear into shape — then
