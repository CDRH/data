      <div class="salute">Dear Comrade: </div>
      
      
      <p>You bet "she's worth it," as you say in yours of the 29th! And a lot more too! I'm so glad she's getting on
         so well.
      </p>
      
      
      <p>Yes, I'll dig up a picture for the surgeon.</p>
      
      
      <p>This is rattled off in a hurry, as you see.</p>
