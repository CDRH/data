      <div class="salute">Dear Comrade: </div>
      
      
      <p>I dictated my note to you on the run.</p>
      
      
      <p>If evaluating two books in one review seems to necessitate sacrificing the article
         as set up - go ahead. Any way you please. Of course the pay is more for two books.
      </p>
      
      
      <p>I'm waiting for a good novel to send to you!</p>
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <div class="salute">Affectionately always </div>
         
         <br></br>Jno.
          
      </div>
