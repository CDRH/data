JOHN G. NEIHARDT

BRANSON, MISSOURI
July 30 1 9 3 2
Dr. U. S. Conn Nebraska State Teachers College Wayne, Nebraska
Dear Dr. Conn:

In my letter mailed yesterday I forgot to say that the copy of BLACK ELK SPEAKS inscribed to you and Mrs. Conn was mailed to you. Mr. Elliott received his copy, and he thought it very likely that your copy was taken to the library by mistake.

Will you please let me know if one of the library copies contains the inscription to you. If you cannot locate it, I will send you another copy.

With kind thoughts,


Jno. G. Neihardt

John G. Neihardt
