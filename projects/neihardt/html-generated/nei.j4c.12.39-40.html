ST. LOUIS POST-DISPATCH

PUBLISHED BY THE PULITZER PUBLISHING CO.

ST. LOUIS, MO.
40
Tuesday
Dear Comrade:

Bully to hear from you. I told you in my last of Hodge's Handbook of American Indians. That will give you information on individuals and their careers.

The "white chief" who went down "feathered like a goose" was undoubtedly Fetterman himself, + you may so state.

Am feeling fairly well again.

Have had our house in Branson made new. Its a dandy. The men have worked 4 weeks on it + it is nearly finished.

Endless love


Jno.
