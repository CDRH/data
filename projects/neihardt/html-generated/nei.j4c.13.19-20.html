ST. LOUIS POST-DISPATCH

PUBLISHED BY THE PULITZER PUBLISHING CO

ST LOUIS, MO
Sept. 15, 1928.
Dear Comrade:

I am to read for Dana Hall Sunday, November 11th, Wellesley, Tuesday November 13th, and Boston City Club and Boston University within two or three days of these dates. Could I get to Boston for Sunday evening engagement if I should stop with you for Friday stunt?

With love always,


Jno.
Did you hear of the new Hall at Wayne State?

"Quest" is being reissued this fall. I suppose "Collected Poems" finished that book, but it seems not. "Wars" out soon — announced.
