                <div class="note">House Collection</div>
<div class="note">May 12, 1926</div>

<span class="dateline">Tuesday</span>

<span class="salute">Dear Comrade:</span>

<p>Robert M. McBride &amp; Co (N.Y.) are publishing a series of "Modern American Writers" biographies, each selling for $1. Cabell, Wharton, Dreiser, Menchen and O'Neill have thus far been issued. I have seen none of these, but judge that a reading of one or two of them (Cabell, O'Neill, I'd say) might help you in deciding upon the form of your next book.</p>

<p>I wanted to make this suggestion while I had it in mind.</p>
<span class="closer">
<span class="salute">Lots of love,</span>

<br/>Jno.
