      <div class="dateline">Bancroft, Aug 11, '20. </div>
      
      
      <div class="salute">Dear Mott: — </div>
      
      
      	
      <p>My outing was a trying business, tho' I appreciated the kindness of the man who took
         me. It was hard not to be ungrateful, I kept thinking how much more profitable it
         would have been to spend some of those hours with you. There wasn't an idea in that
         car nor at Okoboji, and I got back horribly fagged.
      </p>
      	
      <p>My wife and mother report a fine visit with you, however, and that's some compensation.</p>
      	
      <p>I wanted to give you something, but was too rushed. Which of
         
         
      </p>
      <h6 class="fw">15</h6>
      <p>
         
         my books would you care to receive from me?
      </p>
      	
      <p>Come again &amp; stay longer, letting me know several weeks ahead so that no one can have
         any prior claims on me.
      </p>
      	
      <p>Good luck!</p>
      
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <div class="salute">Yours, </div>
         
         
         <br></br>Jno. Neihardt
         
          
      </div>
      
      
      
      <p>Have just heard from two Harvard students who want to come next week  Prof. Turner
         sent them, it seems.
      </p>
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <br></br>—J. N.
