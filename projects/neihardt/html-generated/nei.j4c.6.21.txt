      <div class="salute">Dear Bookfellows: — </div>
      
      	
      <p>I must thank you for October's bookly joy! It is indeed a joy, and I share Starret's
         gratitude. If I knew just where to find them, I would gladly collect some 
         
         
      </p>
      <p>
         <blockquote>
            <p>
               
               <p>" — maiden's hearts and sudden widow's eyes"</p>
               
            </p>
         </blockquote>
      </p>
      <p>
         to be added to our poet's "thirty sacks".
      </p>
      	
      <p>Golly! What a line that is! I must know more about Starret.</p>
      
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <div class="salute">Yours, </div>
         
         
         <br></br>Jno. G. Neihardt
          
      </div>
