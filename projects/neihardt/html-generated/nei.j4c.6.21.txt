<div class="note">Seymour Collection</div>



<span class="dateline">Bancroft, Nebraska, <br/>
Oct. 5, '20.</span>

<span class="salute">Dear Bookfellows: —</span>

	<p>I must thank you for October's bookly joy! It is indeed a joy, and I share Starret's gratitude. If I knew just where to find them, I would gladly collect some 

<blockquote><p>
<p>" — maiden's hearts and sudden widow's eyes"</p>
</p></blockquote>
to be added to our poet's "thirty sacks".</p>
	<p>Golly! What a line that is! I must know more about Starret.</p>

<span class="closer">
<span class="salute">Yours,</span>

<br/>Jno. G. Neihardt
</span>
