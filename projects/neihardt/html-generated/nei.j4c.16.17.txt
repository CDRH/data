      <div class="dateline">Feb 22 '32 </div>
      
      
      
      <div class="salute">Dear Comrade: </div>
      
      
      <p>One more comment on your Coolidge. You felt, evidently, as I did, that the last column
         of his letter was perhaps a bit over eloquent; but you can tone such things down if
         you should use more of his stuff. I just want you to know that I felt about this much
         as you did. There is a wallop in the man, I know.
      </p>
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <div class="salute">Love always - if there be an always for us </div>
         
         
         <br></br>Jno.
          
      </div>
