WESTERN HISTORICAL MANUSCRIPTS COLLECTION

UNIVERSITY OF MISSOURI

COLUMBIA, MO.
SKYRIM FARM

ROUTE 7

COLUMBIA, MISSOURI
12-29-57
Dear Slade Kendrick:

We haven't communicated often of late, but you are in my thoughts very often, and you are dear to me — one of the very dearest of friends. I just want to say hello, and bless your heart! Bless both of you equally in that house of yours where I was happy! Mona and I share the feeling here expressed.

Yours always,


John Neihardt
