Editorial Department

ST. LOUIS POST-DISPATCH

PUBLISHED BY THE PULITZER PUBLISHING CO.

ST. LOUIS, MO.
Wednesday.
Dear Comrade:

Dr. Brown has suddenly decided not to reset the WARS for the school edition. I think the reason is that they are thinking of saving the expense of resetting with a view to advertising the school editions. If that is the idea, it is certainly a good one. Brown wants me to send the Notes as soon as possible, and so I am wondering how soon you can send me any additional material that may seem necessary after you have gone over the text again.

Endless love,


Jno

If you note any typographical errors in text, please tell me.
