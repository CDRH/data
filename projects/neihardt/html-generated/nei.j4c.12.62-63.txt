Editorial Department            ST. LOUIS POST-DISPATCH
<br/>
ST. LOUIS, MO.
                </h6>

<span class="dateline">Sunday</span>

<span class="salute">Dear Comrade:</span>

<p>I'm rushing out a lot of wail, but I want to add to my letter of yesterday as follows: I am absolutely sympathetic with the rabble in the matter of economic + human rights, + if a row should ever come, I shall not on the side of the machine guns. But I am absolutely opposed to the rabble when it monkeys with values it can not understand.</p>

<p>I feel sure you will 
<span class="hr"> </span><span class="pageimage"><a href="large/nei.j4c.12.62-63_0002-2a.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]" title="&lt;a href=&#34;large/nei.j4c.12.62-63_0002-2a.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;"><img src="thumbnails/nei.j4c.12.62-63_0002-2a.jpg" class="display &#xA;            "/></a></span>
<span class="h6">63</span>    
    be with me on this. Vulgar minds can not make my esthetic + ethical values for me. And remember what I said about the "cleavage".</p>
<span class="closer">
<span class="salute">With love always,</span>

<br/>Jno
</span>
