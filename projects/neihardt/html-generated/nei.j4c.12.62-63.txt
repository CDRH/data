      <div class="salute">Dear Comrade: </div>
      
      
      <p>I'm rushing out a lot of wail, but I want to add to my letter of yesterday as follows:
         I am absolutely sympathetic with the rabble in the matter of economic + human rights,
         + if a row should ever come, I shall not on the side of the machine guns. But I am
         absolutely opposed to the rabble when it monkeys with values it can not understand.
      </p>
      
      
      <p>I feel sure you will 
         
         
      </p>
      <h6 class="fw">63</h6>
      <p>    
         be with me on this. Vulgar minds can not make my esthetic + ethical values for me.
         And remember what I said about the "cleavage".
      </p>
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <div class="salute">With love always, </div>
         
         
         <br></br>Jno
