      <div class="salute">Dear Comrade: </div>
      
      
      <p>X (   Please apply at once for copyright in my name.</p>
      
      
      <p>This is, of course, only the preparation of the background for the tragedy.</p>
      
      
      <p>The theme of the Song of the Messiah is this: Spiritual values triumph in this world
         only through defeat.
      </p>
      
      
      <p>A corollary to the proposition, proved over and over in history is: Whenever spiritual
         values appear to triumph through worldly victory, only an empty form remains.
      </p>
      
      
      <p>The Oglalas are preparing for our spring pow-wows. Much evidence of intense interest
         up there. Can hardly wait until spring - want to go now. It will be a wonderful experience,
         for I can feel that everything is shaping up just as I would have it.
      </p>
