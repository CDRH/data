      <h6 class="fw">43</h6>
      
      
      <div class="dateline">Aug 17, '14 </div>
      
      
      <div class="salute">My dear Mr. Sinclair:- </div>
         
      
      <p>In response to yours of the 10th inst., I would suggest the following poems from my
         last two volumes of verse:
      </p>
      
      
      <ul>   
         
         <li>
            <strong>In "Man-Song" (Kennerley) - </strong>
            <br></br>
            
            Czologosz. (page 34)
         </li>
         
         
         <li>
            <strong>In "The Stranger at the Gate" (Kennerley) - </strong>
            <br></br>
            
            Money (page 63)
         </li>
         
         
         <li>The Red Wind Comes (page 64)</li>
         
         
         <li>The Cry of the People (page 66)</li>
         
         
      </ul>
      
      
      <p>The sonnet "Money" seems to me to be especially significant.</p>
      
      
      <p>With best wishes,</p>
      
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <div class="salute">Fraternally, </div>
         
         
         <br></br>Jno. G. Neihardt
