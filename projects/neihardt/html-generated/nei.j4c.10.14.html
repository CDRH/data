Branson, Missouri, March, 6, '25.
My dear Rev. Seymour:-

In re dett recently concurred.

Yours of the 2nd instantaneously received and contents suspected, together with highly partial submittance for same.

Would say that upon having asseverated the present rate of exchange pertainitive to cancelled postage stamps as originally emitted by the Burmese and Canadian Governments. I will accordeonly give you full credence for the respective amount so realized. In the interim, meanwhile, I will open gesticulations for the floating of a moratorium on the uncancelled stamps of identical emission, and will reform you completely as to any eventuations that may perspire.

Thanking you for your generous effort to repudiate your dett at this time.

With all presentiments, etc.,
Yours,


Dr. Neihardt, Litt. D. etc.

Dr. Neihardt
ABC:DEF

This is a business letter, a social letter will follow.



Prof. N.
