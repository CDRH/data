      <h6 class="fw">19</h6>
      
      
      <div class="dateline">March 2, '32 </div>
      
      
      
      <div class="salute">Dear Comrade: </div>
      
      
      <p>I'm wondering if the Black Elk book has reached you. It was to have been sent to you
         more than two weeks ago. I've been doing some lecturing and the crowds all came over
         in a way that surprises me. Was at Dr. Burris Jenkin's Community Church in Kansas
         City last Sunday evening. Big crowd. It stayed with me two hours, an hour longer than
         I wanted, &amp; Jenkins said — it would have sat till midnight, though. That is a well-meant
         exaggeration, I suppose. I wish such things could give me some happiness.
      </p>
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <div class="salute">Endless love, good comrade! </div>
         
         
         <br></br>Jno.
          
      </div>
      
      
      
      <p>
         <u>Are you well</u>?
      </p>
