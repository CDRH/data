      <div class="salute">Dearest Comrade: </div>
      
      
      <p>Don't worry about this moment in a silly but necessary social experiment. Of course
         the rabble isn't educable.
      </p>
      
      
      <p>We are doing something - you and I - that will outlast this twilight.</p>
      
      
      <p>Signs of the growing success of the scheme are increasing.</p>
      
      
      <p>I'm glad you're in the world with me.</p>
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <div class="salute">Endless love, </div>
         
         
         <br></br>Jno.
