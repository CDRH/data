UNDER SCHIMMEL DIRECTION

HOTEL CUSTER

GALESBURG, ILL.
Nov. 16, '39.
Dear Friends:

Surely you are that, and it gave me joy to hear from you.

I've been here as Honnold Lecturer for three weeks — at Knox College — and am completing the series this afternoon. I'll be at home one day, and then Sigurd and I will be off again on a long flight through the West, ending at Christmas. We toured the Old South before I came here.

I've read much of the material you sent me — not all. How you do know, you devoted ones! I'll find a lull on the Western trip and will write more. A-class is waiting for me.

I'm glad you are teaching together again.

IN OMAHA, NEBRASKA

BLACKSTONE HOTEL
(over)
