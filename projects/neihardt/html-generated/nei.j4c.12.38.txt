      <h6 class="fw">38</h6>
                      
      
      <div class="dateline">Thursday </div>
      
      
      <div class="salute">Dear Comrade: </div>
      
      
      <p>It's a shame I have not answered you sooner. I've been sick with the grip and with
         two cracked ribs, and have too much work at the office. Shall try to escape before
         next year if possible.
      </p>
      
      
      <p>For Indian biographies, get 
         <u>Hodge's Handbook of American Indians</u> from the Library. It is in 2 vols, + will give practically all you want on that head.
      </p>
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <div class="salute">Endless love, </div>
         
         
         <br></br>Jno.
