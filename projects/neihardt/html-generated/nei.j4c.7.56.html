Seymour Collection
Nov. 1921
56
718 Webster St.

Palo Alto, Calif.
Dear Seymour:—

Please do not let the "Address" go to press until corrected proof has been received from me. There are a number of rather bad errors, and I want to add a paragraph. I wish you had sent duplicate proofs. As it is I absolutely have to keep the one you sent me.

I told you the other day to send proof to Moscow Idaho; but I think now it would be much better to send to me % Whitman College, Walla Walla, Wash. I'll be there on Nov. 28.

Yours always


Jno G. Neihardt
