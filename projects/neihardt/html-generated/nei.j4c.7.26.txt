      <div class="note">Seymour Collection</div>
      
      <h6 class="fw">26</h6>
      
      <div class="note">ansd</div>
      
      
      
      <div class="dateline">Branson, Missouri, May 20, '21. </div>
      
      
      <div class="salute">Dear Seymour:— </div>
      
      
      
      <p>"Pierrot, Bookfellow" is bully. Is it intended for a bookplate? I judge so. I wish
         it had been offered for the Pack.
      </p>
      
      
      <p>Will Mosher publish the illuminated book you mention? If so, I shall have a copy.</p>
      
      
      <p>Thank Mrs. Avery for letting me read the poem.</p>
      
      
      <p>When you get time, I wish you'd tell me about the banquet.</p>
      
      
      <div class="closer">
         
         <div class="salute">Always yours, </div>
         
         
         <br></br>Jno. N.
          
      </div>
      
      
      
      <p>Did you see the Literary Digest for May 14? The laureate business has aroused lots
         of comment.
      </p>
