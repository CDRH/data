<span class="dateline">Branson, Aug 9 '27</span>

<span class="salute">Dear Comrade:</span>
 
<p>I've been <u>very</u> lazy, deliberately so, for nearly 3 wks. I will drive back to St. Louis Friday.</p>

<p><em class="hi">Yes</em>, you are right. You will write a fine book about the whirl called Neihardt + the work. Also, nothing can stop this thing. The work will last beyond all but a very few things of our generation. This delay is essential, and will lead to
    <span class="h6">31</span>
    <span class="hr"> </span><span class="pageimage"><a href="large/nei.j4c.12.31-32_0001a.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]" title="&lt;a href=&#34;large/nei.j4c.12.31-32_0001a.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;"><img src="thumbnails/nei.j4c.12.31-32_0001a.jpg" class="display &#xA;            "/></a></span>
    <span class="h6">-2-</span>
    greater things as you will see later.</p>

<p>I have signed for year after next with the Affiliated Lyceums of Cleveland. Am now about to become national director of a new + very promising scheme. (I plan to work you in. Shall wait for more definite developments before writing you.</p>

<p>You <u>must</u> be with Macmillans, if possible. But a year or two or three may bring out better ways 
    <span class="hr"> </span><span class="pageimage"><a href="large/nei.j4c.12.31-32_0002a.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]" title="&lt;a href=&#34;large/nei.j4c.12.31-32_0002a.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;"><img src="thumbnails/nei.j4c.12.31-32_0002a.jpg" class="display &#xA;            "/></a></span>
    <span class="h6">32</span>
    Bully for the notes to the <u>Wars!</u> Make them copious enough to be fairly fool-proof with crude teachers. Ask me any questions and I'll reply at once.</p>

<p><u>The best is yet to be with us, Comrade</u>!</p>
<span class="closer">
<span class="salute">Love always,</span>

<br/>Jno.
