<span class="salute">Dear Seymour:—</span>


<p>I have received proofs of the address in plenty of time. I'll have to hold this copy, as I intend to read the lecture a half dozen times between now and Dec. 9. If you think the delay unwise, you might have an extra copy of proof sent to me % prof. H. C. Dale, The University Moscow, Idaho.</p>
