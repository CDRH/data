<span class="dateline">Bancroft, Nebraska, Nov. 9, '19.</span>

Mr. George S. Seymour,
Chicago.

<span class="salute">Dear Friend: —</span>


<p>You may use any of my lyrics in "The Quest" for your Christmas booklet. I would say that either of the following would be most suitable:</p>

<ul><li>The Story (which should be called "The Ballad of a Child")</li><li>Hymn Before Birth.</li><li>The Child's Heritage.</li></ul>
<span class="closer">
<span class="salute">Very truly,</span>
<br/>John G. Neihardt
