                <span class="salute">Dear Slade Kendrick:</span>
                
                <p>We haven't communicated often of late, but you are in my thoughts very often, and you are dear to me — one of the very dearest of friends. I just want to say hello, and bless your heart! <u>Bless both of you equally in that house of yours where I was happy!</u> Mona and I share the feeling here expressed.</p>
                <span class="closer">
                <span class="salute">Yours always,</span>
                <br/>John Neihardt
