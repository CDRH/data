Bancroft, Nebraska, April 19, '11.
Thomas B. Mosher, Esq., Portland, Maine.
Dear Sir:—

Several months ago Mr. Mitchell Kennerley wrote me that you had decided to bring out a number of The Bibelot containing a selection from my lyrics. Kindly advise me as to when this number may be expected.

Very truly yours,


Jno. G. Neihardt
