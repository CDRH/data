Branson, Mo., Feb. 24, '21.
Dear Seymour:—

Enclosed find a dollar bill, for which please enter Judge Vinzenhaler, Branson, Mo., as a member of the Order of Bookfellows. I have not his initials and am not quite certain that my spelling of his name is correct. You will get these details from him.

He is 1070
Yours always,


Jno. N.

Many thanks for your kind letter received day before yesterday. If I come to Chicago, I'll certainly find you and Mrs. Seymour.

Its good to know that you will issue something more by Cabell. I'm strong for him. Have just read "Beyond Life".
