    August 6, 1913
    Mr. H. M. Gerrans,
    Hotel Iroquois,
    Buffalo, N. Y.
    Dear Mr. Gerrans;

    Your telegram, instructing us to draw on Mr. Bleistein for $1500.00, at hand. We have drawn this morning, making a cash item of it. The $2500.00 draft which Mr. Bleistein made on us has not [been presented], or has not reached Cody yet, but we presume it will be within a day or two and we will take care of it.

    Very truly yours,
