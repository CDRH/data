   <p>Civil Government Day Menu, from dinner held on October 14, 1898, as part of Peace Jubilee Week. Printed with blue ink on white stock, with gold edging. U.S. Eagle embossed in gold at top with with colorized shield. Text reads as follows: "Civil Government Day. Bouillon En Tasse, Celery, Broiled Pompano, Maitre D'Hotel, Radishes, Olives, Filet of Beef, Pique Mushrooms, French Peas, Sauterne, Constitutional Punch, Roast Chicken, Au Cresson, Lettuce Salad, Neapolitan Ice Cream, Cakes, Fruits, Coffee, Cigars, Friday, October 14th. Trans-Mississippi and International Exposition."</p>
