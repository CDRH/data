   <p>Aspersions on character. Notarized document attesting to truthfulness of information prepared by G. C. Porter regarding the character of Gurdon W. Wattles. Prepared for Edward Rosewater for Omaha Bee newspaper investigation.  Gives details as to why investigation was opened, and how the claims Porter intially gathered against in Wattles turned out to be unsubstantiated. 9 sheets previously folded in quarters. Typed in blue ink on cream colored paper with red margin lines. Notarized by H. A. Olerich.</p>
