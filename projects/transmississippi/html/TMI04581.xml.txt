   <p>Letter to John A. Wakefield from Lewis S. Reed regarding donation of pictures from the Trans-Mississippi &amp; International Exposition to the City of Omaha. States intention to fit up third floor of Omaha Public Library building to commemorate the exposition. One room will be an art gallery; one room will serve as a musueum. 1 sheet previously folded in twelfths.</p>
